Your Privacy Matters

We believe in complete transparency about how your data is handled.

No Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any information about your browsing activity, blocked content, or extension usage.
No Analytics or Telemetry
The extension does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking code. Zero external connections for data collection.
No Third-Party Sharing
Your data is never sold, shared, or transferred to any third party. Period.
100% Local Processing
All blocking decisions, statistics, and settings are processed and stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your browser.
Open Filter Lists
We use well-known, community-maintained filter lists (EasyList, EasyPrivacy, uBlock). The only network requests are to download these public lists.
